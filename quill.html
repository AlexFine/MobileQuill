Quill takes notes for you

Quill analyzes your text into a data visualization, takes notes for you and allows you to share and impact those notes with your peers. On our mobile app, you take a picture of your text, we take notes on it and summarize it. For our web version, we also provide data visiualizations and multipart upload.

Our Solution

Upload Text

Start by uploading the text you want analyzed.

Analyze Text

We will analyze the text and provide notes, summaries and data visualizations about the text.

Crowd Source Data

If you don't find what you want, feel free to add your own note. You can also upvote and downvote notes so all of our content is curated to you.

Data Visualization

We provide a data visualization to help you understand the text in a new and different way.
